Project 2

In this exciting project, I leveraged advanced machine learning techniques to develop a highly accurate banknote authentication system. Utilizing the power of Random Forest Classifiers, I meticulously tuned the model parameters through an extensive GridSearchCV process to identify the optimal settings. With features extracted from real-world data, the final model achieved an exceptional accuracy rate, demonstrating its efficacy in distinguishing between genuine and counterfeit banknotes.

Project 3

Introducing my innovative Investment Portfolio Dashboard, an advanced tool designed to provide a dynamic and interactive analysis of your investment portfolio's performance and risk. This dashboard empowers you with the ability to input various assets and their respective weights, ensuring accurate and personalized analysis. It validates asset symbols, retrieves historical adjusted close prices, and calculates daily returns, providing comprehensive data insights. The tool features interactive visualizations, comparing your portfolio's performance against the S&P 500 benchmark with sleek line charts, and displaying your asset allocation with a dark-themed pie chart. Detailed risk metrics for both your portfolio and the benchmark help you understand your portfolio's volatility relative to the market, accompanied by insightful commentary on the risk profile. Built using Python, yFinance, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Streamlit, this user-friendly dashboard transforms complex financial analysis into an accessible and engaging experience, enabling you to make informed investment decisions with clarity and confidence.
